# tr3m
# tr3m AI is a highly polished, single-file serverless web application designed to strip away robotic AI patterns from your copy and convert raw scripts into high-performance, viral short-form video assets (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels).

Equipped with dual-language auto-detection, a tailored Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine optimizer, and real-time viral auditing, this tool is the ultimate pipeline for modern digital creators, copywriters, and growth hackers.

✨ Key Features

1. 🦾 AI Humanizer (Natural Rhythm Calibration)

Zero AI Footprint: Completely filters out typical robotic AI cliches ("In conclusion", "Moreover", "Furthermore", "Özetle", "Buna ek olarak").

Density Lock: Enforces a strict near-1:1 character ratio constraint. No more over-inflated fluff paragraphs or overly summarized cuts.

Burstiness Tuning: Mimics organic human writing patterns by varying sentence structures and structural rhythms.

2. 🎙️ Smart TTS (Voiceover) Optimizer

Intelligent Expansion: Expands text shorthand and conversational acronyms (e.g., btw $\rightarrow$ by the way, vb. $\rightarrow$ ve benzeri) to prevent AI voiceover engine stuttering.

Hardware & Brand Safe-list: Explicitly protects globally recognized trade names, silicon standards, and developer terminology (e.g., TSMC, CUDA cores, Ryzen AI) so they remain perfectly untouched.

3. 🔥 Viral Video Pack Generator

Algorithmic Scoring: Calculates an interactive 100-point viral potential score based on immediate audience retention parameters.

Growth Hacker Critique: Generates actionable, sharp psychological audits highlighting curiosity gaps and narrative hooks.

High-CTR Title Suite: Delivers 3 clickable, high-impact titles ready for copy-paste.

Metadata Stack: Generates algorithm-friendly descriptions, targeted trending hashtags, and high-volume hidden keywords (Tags) to boost search engine discoverability.

4. 🔒 Privacy-First Serverless Architecture

No database, no third-party tracking, and absolutely no external server logs.

Your Google AI Studio API key runs entirely in your local browser runtime via localStorage.
